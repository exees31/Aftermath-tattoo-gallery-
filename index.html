Did You Know?

Tattoo Facts

5,000

Years old — The oldest known tattoos were found on Otzi the Iceman, a mummy dating back over 5,000 years. He had 61 tattoos, mostly simple lines and dots.

🗿
100+

Needle punctures per second — A tattoo machine punctures your skin between 50 and 150 times per second, depositing ink into the dermis layer just below the surface.

⚡
1/3

Of adults have a tattoo — Approximately one in three adults in Western countries now has at least one tattoo, making it one of the most widespread art forms in modern culture.

📊
2mm

Depth of ink placement — Tattoo ink is deposited into the dermis, roughly 1–2mm below the skin surface. Too shallow and it fades; too deep and it blurs over time.

🔬
∞

Why tattoos are permanent — Your immune system tries to remove the ink, but the particles are too large to absorb. They stay trapped in skin cells — permanently.

🧬
1891

Year the electric tattoo machine was patented — Samuel O'Reilly patented the first electric tattoo machine in New York, based on Thomas Edison's electric pen design.

⚙️
40%

Of people regret at least one tattoo — But most don't regret getting tattooed at all — just the specific design. That's why consulting with a great artist matters so much.

💭
17

Days of peak healing — The top layer of skin flakes and peels within the first 2–3 weeks. Don't pick it — let it heal naturally for the best result.
